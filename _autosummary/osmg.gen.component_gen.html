Returns the necessary offset to connect an element at a specified snap point of the section.

Parameters:

  • placement – Placement tag. Can be any of “centroid”, “top_center”, “top_left”, “top_right”, “center_left”, “center_right”, “bottom_center”, “bottom_left”, “bottom_right”

  • section – Section object.

  • p_i – Internal point at the i-end.

  • p_j – Internal point at the j-end.

Returns:

The offset.
