.. py:class:: Section

   Section object.
   The axes are defined in the same way as they are
   defined in OpenSees. The colors assigned to
   the axes for plotting follow the
   AutoCAD convention.

           y(green)
           ^         x(red)
           :       .
           :     .
           :   .
          ===
           | -------> z (blue)
          ===
